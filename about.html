About Us

Data Safari Academy is a free and accessible platform dedicated to empowering young minds in Kenya and beyond by providing top-tier data science education. Our team embarked on a mission to develop an innovative Data Science Learning Platform, aimed at equipping aspiring and existing data scientists with the right resources. We conducted an extensive community outreach initiative across the country, engaging with students at different academic levels—those exploring career paths and those already in the tech field. Our goal was to create a bridge between curiosity and expertise, making data science approachable for all.

Our research focused on understanding perceptions of Data Science, its career prospects, and how learners prefer to acquire knowledge. We also analyzed interests in Data Science Hackathons, bootcamps, and available industry opportunities. The findings played a crucial role in shaping the design and functionality of this website. We meticulously crafted an interactive and user-friendly learning environment, ensuring that it meets the diverse needs of aspiring data scientists. From rural polytechnics to urban universities, we’ve tailored our content to resonate with learners from all walks of life, fostering a community where knowledge is shared freely and innovation thrives.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every young person, regardless of background, has the opportunity to explore and excel in data science—a field that’s shaping the world through technology and innovation. By making learning fun, interactive, and inclusive, we aim to cultivate the next generation of data-driven problem solvers. Our platform isn’t just about teaching skills; it’s about inspiring a mindset of exploration and resilience. We see Data Safari Academy as a catalyst for change, breaking down barriers to STEM education and empowering individuals to contribute to a data-driven future, both locally in Kenya and globally.

Our Journey

The journey to build Data Safari Academy began with a simple question: How can we make data science education accessible to everyone? Our team traveled across Kenya, from Nairobi’s bustling tech hubs to the quiet classrooms of Kisumu and Eldoret, gathering insights from students, educators, and industry professionals. We hosted workshops, conducted surveys, and listened to stories of ambition and challenge. These experiences shaped our platform’s core features—mobile-friendly design, offline resources, and a focus on practical, hands-on learning. Every line of code, every lesson plan, and every design choice reflects our commitment to those we met along the way.
